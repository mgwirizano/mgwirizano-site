Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is my Member ID and where do I find it?
Your Member ID was sent to your registered email when you joined. It looks like MW0001. You can also find it using the Member Portal or Help Centre chatbot.
How do I make a payment?
All payments are made via Zelle to mgwirizano2026@gmail.com. Always include your Member ID in the memo — e.g. MW0001 Registration Fee or MW0001 FUND-001.
Why didn't I receive a payment confirmation?
Check your spam/junk folder for an email from mgwirizano2026@gmail.com. Confirmations are sent within 15 minutes. If nothing after 1 hour, email us with your Member ID and amount.
What family members are covered?
Coverage is strictly biological: biological father, biological mother, legally married spouse, biological children, and biological siblings (including half-siblings who share at least one biological parent). Stepsiblings with no shared biological parent and adopted family members do not qualify.
What happens if I miss contributions?
Missing 2 consecutive funds places you AT RISK. Missing 3 consecutive funds marks you INELIGIBLE. You can restore eligibility by contributing to the next 3 consecutive funds.
How long until I receive the funeral fund money?
Funds are disbursed promptly after the fund closing date. 95% goes directly to the bereaved family. 5% is allocated to the Administration Fund for operating costs. A personal message from leadership accompanies every disbursement.
How do I report a bereavement?
Use the Member Self-Service Form (linked above under Resources). Select "Report a Bereavement." You will receive an instant condolence email and be automatically exempted from active funds.
Where are the financial statements?
Quarterly financial statements are published on the Reports page every quarter. All disbursements, income, and balances are fully disclosed. No secrets, ever.
